Trade Engine Website - Build Plan
Project: Trade Engine (https://tradeengine.io) Purpose: Marketing website to sell AI-powered white-label websites to trade businesses, starting with landscaping. Pricing: £4,995 setup + £199/month

Brand Identity
* Primary Color: Deep blue (#1e40af) - trust, professionalism
* Secondary Color: Electric blue (#3b82f6) - innovation, tech
* Accent Color: Vibrant green (#10b981) - growth, success, trades
* Style: Modern SaaS, clean white backgrounds, bold typography
* Tone: Professional but approachable, results-focused

Site Structure (Single Page with Sections)
1. Navigation
* Logo (Trade Engine)
* Links: Features, How It Works, Demo, Pricing, Contact
* CTA Button: "Book a Demo"
2. Hero Section
* Headline: "AI-Powered Websites That Convert Leads Into Customers"
* Subheadline: "Give your trade business an unfair advantage with instant quotes, AI design visualization, and automated follow-ups - all white-labeled to your brand."
* Primary CTA: "See It In Action" (links to Premium Landscapes demo)
* Secondary CTA: "Book a Call"
* Hero image/video: Screenshot or animation of the quote system
3. Problem/Solution Section
* "Your competitors are still using contact forms. You'll be closing deals."
* Pain points: Slow quote turnaround, lost leads, no follow-up, outdated websites
* Solution: AI-powered instant engagement
4. Features Breakdown (with icons/visuals)
Feature cards with expandable details:
a) AI Instant Quote System
* 6-step conversational form
* Dual mode: Full project quotes OR individual product selection
* Real-time price calculations
* PDF quotes generated automatically
* "Customers get pricing in 2 minutes, not 2 days"
b) AI Garden Visualization
* Upload photo → AI transforms it into design preview
* Delivered via email in 90 seconds
* Based on customer's actual requirements
* "Let customers SEE their new garden before you build it"
c) Automated CRM & Follow-ups
* Every lead captured automatically
* WhatsApp & email follow-up sequences
* Integration with Airtable/Zoho CRM
* "Never lose a lead again"
d) White-Label Ready
* Your logo, colors, contact details
* Your domain
* Fully branded PDF quotes
* "Looks like you spent £20k on development"
e) n8n Workflow Automation
* Quote routing based on project type
* Automated notifications
* Pricing logic from your spreadsheet
* "Set it and forget it"
5. Live Demo Section
* Embedded iframe or prominent link to Premium Landscapes demo
* "Try the exact system your customers will use"
* Callouts highlighting key features as they explore
6. How It Works (Timeline)
1. Discovery Call - We understand your business and services
2. Setup (1 Week) - We configure everything with your branding
3. Training - Quick walkthrough of your new system
4. Go Live - Start converting more leads immediately
5. Ongoing Support - We handle updates and optimizations
7. Pricing Section
* Setup: £4,995 one-time
    * Includes: Full website, AI quote system, AI design generator, CRM integration, automation workflows, white-labeling, training
* Monthly: £199/month
    * Includes: Hosting, maintenance, AI credits, support, updates
* CTA: "Book Your Demo"
* Note: "ROI after just 2-3 extra jobs per month"
8. FAQ Section
* How long does setup take?
* Do I need technical knowledge?
* What if I want changes?
* How does the AI design work?
* What CRM systems do you integrate with?
* Can I see a live example?
9. Testimonials/Social Proof
* Placeholder for client quotes
* Stats: "Average 40% increase in quote requests"
* Logos of integrations (n8n, Make, Airtable, WhatsApp, etc.)
10. Final CTA Section
* "Ready to transform your trade business?"
* Book a Demo button
* "Or call us: [phone number]"
11. Footer
* Trade Engine logo
* Links: Features, Pricing, Demo, Contact, Privacy Policy
* Contact details
* Social links
* Copyright

* Single-page scrolling design
* Mobile-first responsive
* Tailwind CSS
* Smooth scroll navigation
* Contact/demo booking form (webhook to CRM)
* Fast loading, optimized images
